Our Restaurant

L'artista was established in 2017 with a view to bring the best of Italian cuisine and fine dining experience to the heart of Letchworth Garden City. In order to accomplish this we set out to make sure our menu was modernised with the authentic Italian feel that would prove to be very successful for our local customers.

You can expect a dining experience that shall leave only the best impression on you when you come to visit L'Artista with customers from every age range catered for. Our atmosphere is well reknowned in the local area and you can expect impeccable customer service with our staff being of exceptional standard.

People come just to try our home-made pizzas, knowing that our dough is freshly prepared in front of you using only the finest ingredients. L'artista has introduced many different varieties of pasta from all over Italy and also a wide variety of typical Italian antipasti (starters) as well as meat and fish dishes. With such a varied and extensive menu, there is something for everyone including Vegetarians. Many of our customers return months after months, years after years and are constantly telling us that the atmosphere is so friendly and casual, 'eating in L'artista feel like 'eating in a pizzeria in Italy while you are in the heart of garden city of Letchworth.
